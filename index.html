Pets in US Narrative Visualization

Scene 1: Pet Households in US -- Number One

What are the top three states with the highest number of pet households is an interesting question. Someone may take a guess. New York? Pennsylvania? Nope. If you are interested in this question, this Pet households narrative visualization has the right information for you.

Let me first define what is “pet” mean here. It simply means ONLY dogs and cats. Other kinds of animals such as birds and snakes are not included in this data.

California has the highest number of household who has pets. This means if one household has only cats, only dogs or both cats and dogs is included here. California is the number one in number of pet households. It has 6,865,000 household with pets in their house. Also, it represents 10.4% of the total pet households in US. Did you guess it right?

Summary:

Martini Glass structure were used in this narrative visualization. The main focus is to answer the question started in the narrative: "What are the top three states with the highest number of pet households?" Scene 1, scene 2 and scene 3 are answering the question one by one with author driven contents which showed the top one, two and three states that has the most pet households. Also, they show the percentage of pet households using D3 Pie Charts with tooltips showing the state, number and percentage of each category. "Jumping off point" is in Scene 3 to lead users to the final scene, scene 4.

Scene 4 contains reader driven content. It allows users to interact with the charts and know more detail on what they are interested in. All the Scenes follow the same template. Also, D3 were used in this Visualization on Scene 4 to offer interactions.

Annotations

Legend of the pie chart were annotated with differernt colors.

Parameters

There are a few parameters used in the scene:

1 Scene number. In this Scene, it is scene 1.

2 Ranking of number of pet households. In this scene, California has the highest number of pet households.

3 States. This parameter is California.

4 In pie chart, it shows and highlights the percentage of the sepcific states mentioned in the scene.

Triggers

When mouse moveover the pie chart, the relative information showed in the tooltip. When mouse move off the pie chart, it disppeared.

According to the course videos, the trigger could be the connections between parameters. So when clicking “scene 1” and change to scene 1, the ranking of the number of pet household, the states, and pie chart, will be changed.

The mouse click acts as a trigger event for navigation.
